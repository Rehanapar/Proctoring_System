Proctoring Exam Instructions

Please read the following instructions carefully before starting your proctored exam:

  • Ensure that your environment is quiet and free of distractions.
  • Do not use your mobile phone during the exam.
  • Do not switch to other tabs or applications during the exam.
  • If any suspicious activity is detected, the interview will be ended immediately.
  • Make sure your camera and microphone are working properly before starting the exam.
  • Ensure that you are sitting in front of the camera at all times during the exam.
  • You are not allowed to communicate with anyone during the exam, unless specified.
  • Do not take screenshots or record the exam content.
  • Be prepared to show your environment and desk to the proctor if required.
  • If you face any technical issues, notify the proctor immediately.
